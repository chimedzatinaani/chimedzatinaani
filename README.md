## About Me

I am a software developer and entrepreneur focused on scaling local tech ecosystems. By blending modern engineering with context-aware product design, I build tools that empower businesses and communities in Southern Africa.

* **Currently Scaling:** WindowShopper, Zimbabwe's premier local shopping and price discovery platform.
* **Background:** Bridging technology and social impact, with extensive experience designing MIS, M&E, and Child Protection data tools at FACT Zimbabwe.
* **Community Driven:** Built tailored digital ecosystem solutions for Africa University students and alumni networks.
